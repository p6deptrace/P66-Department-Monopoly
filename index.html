Each month, Division winners can choose from a variety of rewards, including:

End-of-Year Grand Prize

The overall top performer for each division will receive the following:

  • Trip to the CoralTree Leadership Forum for professional development and networking
  • $1,000 cash prize
  • Two additional nights at the resort

Motivation & Engagement
